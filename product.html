Behold the Perfect Waterbottle!

Traditional bottles are like blankets (they just trap the cold). The "Perfect Bottle" is like a portable refrigerator. It uses a tiny piece of technology called a Peltier Chip (Thermoelectric Cooler). How it works: When electricity from a small rechargeable battery flows through this chip, one side gets freezing cold while the other side gets hot. The Function: The cold side is pressed against the metal lining of your water, sucking the heat out of your drink and dropping the temperature by 10-20 degrees in minutes! The Exhaust: To keep the bottle from overheating, the bottom has tiny "heat sinks" (metal fins) and a silent micro-fan to blow the warmth away.
